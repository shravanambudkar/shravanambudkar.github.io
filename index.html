Shravan is currently a research analyst at Pixxel Space, working under the guidance of Dr. Logan A. Wright. His work focuses on developing groundbreaking Machine Learning methodologies using satellite and airborne data for environmental monitoring. He also serves as an external consultant for Forecastro, specializing in predicting climate change through the integration of AI and IoT. Additionally, he collaborates closely with the Data Science research community at ThoughtWorks, particularly with Shraddha Surana, focusing on reasoning for AI.

Shravan graduated with distinction from Symbiosis Skills and Professional University, India, with a degree in Mechatronics Engineering. During his undergraduate studies, he had the opportunity to work under the guidance of Prof. J. Adinarayana and his PhD student, Dr. Rahul Raj, at the IIT Bombay-CSRE lab, contributing to research in Agro-Informatics using Data Science.

Shravan's primary research focus lies in machine learning, with a particular emphasis on creating innovative, data-driven methods that intersect machine learning, computer vision, and multi-modal data. His goal is to address open questions in environmental monitoring and science through interdisciplinary approaches. He is especially interested in employing diverse methodologies to monitor and assess environmental systems, aiming to contribute valuable insights to environmental science and sustainability. Additionally, Shravan is also interested in developing models that are explainable and capable of reasoning in highly influential contexts, such as scientific computing and environmental policy decisions.
